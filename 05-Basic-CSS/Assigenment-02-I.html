Chocolate Brownie Cake

List of Ingredients

Dry Ingredients Wet Ingredients
1 cup all-purpose flour (maida) ½ cup melted butter
¾ cup cocoa powder (unsweetened) 1 cup sugar
1 tsp baking powder 2 eggs (or yogurt as egg substitute)
A pinch of salt 1 tsp vanilla essence
Optional: Choco chips, chopped nuts ½ cup milk (as needed)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven
    • Set your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Grease a baking pan with butter or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
    • In a bowl, sift together: Flour, Cocoa powder, Baking powder, Salt.
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients
    • In another bowl:
    • Add melted butter + sugar
    • Mix well until creamy
    • Add eggs (or yogurt) and vanilla essence
    • Mix again until smooth
  4. Combine Wet + Dry
    • Slowly add the dry mix into the wet mix.
    • Stir gently using a spatula or whisk.
    • Add milk little by little to adjust consistency (it should be thick but pourable).
    • Optional: Fold in choco chips or nuts.
  5. Bake
    • Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
    • Tap the pan gently to remove air bubbles.
    • Bake for 25–30 minutes at 180°C (until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs).
  6. Cool & Serve
    • Let it cool completely before cutting.
    • Slice into squares and enjoy!
  7. Serving Tips
    • Dust with powdered sugar or cocoa.
    • Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
    • Drizzle melted chocolate or ganache on top for extra richness.
